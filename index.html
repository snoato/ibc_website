Botballers improving Botball

The IBC is an open coalition of Botball teams with the aim to improve the Botball experience for every team. We consider ourselves an body of representation for the interests of Botball teams.

Who are we?

IBC is an open Botball students and team association. We represent member and non-member teams alike.

What is our Mission?

We want to represent our interests and work together with the organizations behind Botball to improve the experience for all Botball students.

Join us!

Every Botball team is free to join the IBC and invited to shape the future of Botball together with us.
